Bolognese

With this simple recipe, you will be able to create a delicious meal.

Best to enjoy it with friends and family

Ingredients:

  • onions
  • carrots
  • celery
  • minced meat
  • canned tomatoes
  • herbs

Steps:

  1. finely dice the onions, carrots and celery
  2. put in a pre heated pot at around medium high heat
  3. put in the minced meat
  4. let everything mix up for a while and then add the canned tomatoes
  5. add the herbs, salt and pepper
  6. let it cook for minimum 2 hours. The longer you cook it the better it gets.
